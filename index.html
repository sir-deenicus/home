Creative Collaboration

What if we could more easily stand on each other's shoulders in Creating Art?

by Deen Abiola

For Example Supposing I create...

  1. A Drawing but I can't write
  2. A Script but I can't animate
  3. A Poem but I can't illustrate
  4. Or Lyrics but I can't compose

I create it, purposefully leave it unfinished

Other people, maybe You

will see what I started

And then maybe, Just maybe YOU are inspired too

To build upon, To add on another Artistic dimension to the Concept

Some will initiate the seeds to the Vision

(A vision belonging to all and none)

Some will contriubute layers to the Vision

Together we create the Final Masterpiece

One Piece at a Time =)

Today we have memes

They are great. Fun Even.

And will likely always have a place on the Net.

But what if we extended the concept

To Create Works of Art that Even the Reneissace Artists...

Would Admire

Anyone, Regardless of Skill Level

should be able to contribute meangingfully

to Magnificence

  • Our tools for Creation And Collaboration
  • For Composition
  • For Remixes, Mix Mashes And Mash ups
  • Could All be So Much BETTER

That is the Dream.

(thank you)
